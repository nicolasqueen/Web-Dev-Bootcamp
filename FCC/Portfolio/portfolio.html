About Me

My name is Nick Queen and I currently work as a licensed social worker but want to make a career change into web development. I have had a long love affair with the web, reaching all the way back to the days when Yahoo was king, news groups were all the rage and ads were few and far between. I had many embarrassing Geocities sites that were unfortunately archived on Oocities. I see the internet and web development as an extension of my career as a social worker in helping the free flow of information and ideas to help others.
